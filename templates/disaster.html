Potential Natural Disaster Alert

Stay informed with reliable news and weather reports. Prepare an emergency kit with essentials.

What to Do:

  1. Get Help: Call the emergency number 4857574839 for immediate assistance and information.
  2. Stay Informed: Keep up with local news, weather updates, and emergency alerts via TV, radio, or mobile apps.
  3. Have an Emergency Kit Ready: Prepare a kit with essentials like water, food, first aid supplies, medications, flashlights, batteries, and important documents.
  4. Create a Family Emergency Plan: Establish communication plans, safe locations, and meeting points in case of separation.
  5. Secure Your Home: Protect your home by boarding up windows, securing outdoor items, and turning off utilities if advised.
  6. Evacuate if Necessary: Follow evacuation orders promptly, know your routes, and take your emergency kit and pets with you.
